About Me

Jonas Davidow is a Kansas City native born and raised now living in Chicago, Illinois for 8 years. He received his high school diploma from Blue Valley North in 2006 and received his four year Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in musical theatre from Drake University.


After graduation Jonas moved to Chicago and started auditioning for shows while working as a leasing agent for Chicago Apartment Finders. Soon he decided he no longer liked driving a car in the city so he switched to a desk job working for Groupon's customer support and eventually working on the customer resolutions team.


Three and a half years later he left Groupon and worked from home as a Mac Plus Senior Support Advisor. Now after working at home for three and a half years he became stir crazy and started working at the Lincoln Park Apple Store in March of 2018 where he's still working and also enrolled in Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp Program (August 2018).
